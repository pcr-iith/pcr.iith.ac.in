Vision

  • Greater visibility and relevance of IITH with key industries and corporates.
  • Expand and improve career opportunities for students by facilitating inustry engagement in curricula, teaching/learning experiences and long-term internships.
  • An alternate approach for placement & internships:
    • Job fairs & New timelines,
    • Inclusion of diverse sector like finance & consulting for recruitment.
  • Career development guidance and services for students and alumni by industry affiliates and experts.
  • Flexible policy for placement in case student opting for entrepreneurship.
  • Support mutually beneficial public policy initiatives through encouraging partnerships with industry and institute.

Key Result Areas

Office of Career Services

  • Placements & Internship - augment the placement Internship & PPOs through robust foundation of Corporate Relations
  • Career Counselling to prepare student to make right choice of career
  • Knowledge sharing sessions by Professionals from reputed organizations
 

www.ocs.iith.ac.in

Corporate Relations & Social Responsibility

  • Foster business engagements to conceptualization, develop & implement strategic initiatives.
  • Regular meeting with Corporates to:
    • Explore CSR opportunities
    • Research collaboration & funding
    • Prospective recruitment for the students

Other Initiatives

  • Activities on campus by companies (i.e. hackathons, webinars etc.).
  • Japan Day - Orientation of Japanese companies for placement & internship at IITH along with Students with the help of JETRO & JICA
  • Felicitation ceremony and Information sharing session between outgoing & incoming student coordinators to share the experience & challenges.
  • Student counsel and time-to- time orientation to take care of student grievances

Guidelines

Corporate relations office acts as a bridge between IIT Hyderabad & industry. It will help companies to identify & access the institute's vast resources which include cutting-edge research, state-of-the-art infrastructure, student recruitment & collaborations. Also, it will help the corporates in continuing education programs for professional development.
