Message from Dean PCR

"The office of Public and Corporate Relations (PCR) facilitates a holistic public and corporate relations network, leveraging the institutes research visibility and brand along with its faculty and student talent. It supports the institutes research and education mission by attracting, engaging, and cultivating a broad spectrum of strategic partnerships with corporates which benefit the student's career development."

- Prof. C Krishna Mohan
